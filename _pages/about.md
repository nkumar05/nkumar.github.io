I am **Naresh Kumar**, an Economist at the World Bank in India. My work focuses on applied economic analysis at the intersection of macroeconomic policy, productivity and structural transformation, and labor market dynamics. My research examines the labor market implications of technological change and AI, the green transition, and the care economy, using empirical methods with a strong emphasis on policy relevance.
